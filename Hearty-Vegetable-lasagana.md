##*Ingredients*
  *1(16 ounce) package lasagna noodles        *2 (26 ounce) jars of pasta sauce 
  *1 pound fresh mushrooms, sliced            *1 teaspoon dried basil 
  *3/4 cup chopped green bell pepper          *1 (15 ounce) container part-skim ricotta
